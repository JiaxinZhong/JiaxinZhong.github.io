Nonlinearity-enabled sound field control

We leveraged the local nonlinearity to enable the deep subwavelength control of acoustic waves. We introduced an approach for creating remote audio spots, dubbed audible enclaves, by harnessing the local nonlinear interaction of two self-bending ultrasonic beams with distinct spectra.

PNAS 2025 | PRL 2023

Directional audio beam generated by parametric array loudspeakers (PALs)

We conducted a series of research on the physical properties of PALs and the advanced beamforming techniques baased on multi-channel PALs.
